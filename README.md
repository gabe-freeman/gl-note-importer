# Gospel Library Note Transfer Tools

A lightweight web extension that injects Import and Export buttons directly into the [Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints notes website](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/notes), allowing you to easily back up your data or migrate your scripture study history from one account to another.

## How to Use It
1. **Exporting from Account**
    * Sign in to the churchofjesuschrist.org profile that currently holds your notes.

    * Navigate to your Notes dashboard (e.g., [https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/notes](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/notes)).

    * Click Export.

    * Uncheck any data types you wish to exclude, then click Export to File. A .json backup file will download to your computer.

2. **Importing to Account**

    * Sign out of the website, then sign in to your other/target account.

    * Return to the Notes dashboard.

    * Click Import.

    * Select the data types you want to import from your file.

    * Click Import from File, choose the .json file you downloaded in Step 1, and wait for the tracking indicator to say complete. Refresh the page to see your synced study history!
